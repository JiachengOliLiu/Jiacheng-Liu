COCO Fresh Tea & Juice Branding

This is a rebranding project for COCO Fresh Tea & Juice, a Taiwanese tea company which is famous for its milk tea and juice. Based on its current visual identity, I redesigned its logo, together with some patterns and images. Also, I applied the graphics in stationeries, menus, posters and packages.

Inspirations

- youngness, energy, freedom -

The basic shapes of the brand logo are the four COCO letters rolling in a square cup, resembling the shape of pearls, which is a very delicious and popular ingredient in COCO’s milk tea. In order to show the brand’s energy and appeal to its target audience - young and energetic people, I applied two emojis and 3 bright colours, as well as a 45 degree rotation and a concise logo font, to make it look vigorous and fashionable.
