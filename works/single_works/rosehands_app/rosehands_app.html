Rosehands App Design

This project is a production of a service design workshop, which was arranged by Tongji University and Design School Kolding. I worked with students from different countries, aiming to design a new service to improve peopleâ€™s life in a new-built community in China. With our research into both old traditional communities and new communities, we found that in new communities, people lack and are eager for more communication and opportunities to interact and help each other. Therefore we decided to design an app, in which people could communicate and make friends in their community.

Research & Data Analysis of Old & New Communities

In order to get a clearer idea of what life is like and what people need in new-built communities, we researched and compared several old and new communities, and found some differences between them, one of which is that people in new communities find it difficult to keep a warm relationship with their neighbours. As a result of this, the residents often feel hard to get help within the neighbourhood, unlike that in old communities where people often help each other. Therefore, we decided to design an app for the residents to seek for and provide help for neighbours, as well as to interact and make friends in their community.

Function & Objective

-A platform for people to seek help & help others in their community-

-Improve communications and interactions of residents-
