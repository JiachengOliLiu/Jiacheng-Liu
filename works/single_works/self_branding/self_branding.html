Self Branding

This is a branding project aiming to promote my self-identity. I had a research of my own personalities, based on which I developed my design. As it is easy to notice, its graphics have been applied in this website to present the identity of Jiacheng Liu.

Ideas

In my research on myself, I figured out that I’m a multi-characteristical person, which couldn’t be represented by merely one logo, so I decide to have one main logo and six sub logos showing the typical aspects of my personality - fun, logic, introvert, music lover, photographer and retro fashion fan.

The basic outline of the main logo comes from my facial appearance - thick hair and glasses. Besides, I have a nickname, ‘Orange’, which is why I used orange as one of the main colours and applied two orange transverses in the logo. The sub logos are all altered on the base of this main logo according to my different characteristics.
