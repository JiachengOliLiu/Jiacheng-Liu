Photography in Egypt

In 2018, I participated Backpacking Egypt, which is an AIESEC volunteering project. I travelled around Egypt with a group of young people from over 10 countries, and the aim of our trip was to photograph the landscapes and people of Egypt and promote the photos in social media in order to promote Egyptian tourism.

The Beauty of Egyptian Landscape

Using a DSLR, I carefully minded my focal length and composition, always trying to choose the best perspective. Also, I paid attention to the colours, lights and shadows, trying to make them cohere well with compositions.
