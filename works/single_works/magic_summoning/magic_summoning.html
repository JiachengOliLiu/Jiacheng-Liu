Animation: The Magic Summoning

This is a branding project aiming to promote my self-identity. I had a research of my own personalities, based on which I developed my design. As it is easy to notice, its graphics have been applied in this website to present the identity of Jiacheng Liu.

Inspiration: The Spirit of Rebellion

This short animation is about a magic girl summoning her servants, but this girl is different with any kind and gentle magic girl we have seen. She is rebellious and wants to be different from others. She dresses differently and use a very unique way to summon - using her wand to blow bubbles and let the bubbles convert to her servants.

Characters

Satannie - The Rebellious Spirit

My heroine, named Satannie and aged around 15 years old, is like an alter ego of myself. In reality, I always do the right thing in the right occasion, but Satannie is different. She is rebellious and evil, having a passion to break the traditions.
