2017 SOA Grad Site

This is a web design & developing project, in which I designed a website to promote the 2017 Graduation Exhibition of School of Art & Design, ANU. I designed the icons, sitemaps, wireframes, interfaces and interactions, and developed the site using html, CSS and javascript.

-Friendly to the Audience-

In order to let the audience quickly find the works they might be interested in, I categorised the works and set sorting filters in the Artists page. Also, to give people a convenient platform to purchase students’ artworks, there is a ‘My Page’ for the convenience of collecting favourite works and purchasing.
