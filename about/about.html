Imagination

Japanese author Yusuke Kishi noted in his novel, From the New World, that, 想像力こそが、全てを変える (Imagination is what changes everything). In Japanese, 想像力(imagination) and 創造力(innovation) share the same pronunciation, and I believe the two conceptions are closely related to each other. They both indicate the ability of making something from the state of 無(nothing) to the state of 有(existing). With both of them, we can make life much more colourful for ourselves as well as for other people.
